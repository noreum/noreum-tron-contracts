Noreum Network

Noreum Network is a sovereign blockchain initiative built around constitutional governance, fixed-supply economics, and progressive decentralization.

Overview

Noreum is designed as a governance-first protocol framework where economic design, security assumptions, and administrative authority are explicitly separated and constrained to support long-term protocol continuity.

Native NOR on the Noreum Layer-1 blockchain (primary sovereign asset)
